Attributes and Features

One More Step Closer to Fire Safety

Live Fire Detection

Harnessing cutting-edge technology, our web app offers real-time fire detection capabilities, analyzing live camera/video streams to swiftly identify and alert users to the presence of flames.

Interactive Image Map

Enhancing user engagement and comprehension, our intuitive interface features an interactive image map, enabling users to click on different parts of the building to access real-time metadata of the environment in that specific location.

Intelligent Forecasting

Leveraging climate data, Flame AI goes beyond reactive measures, proactively identifying regions susceptible to rapid fire growth or escalation, allowing for preemptive measures and strategic resource allocation.

Instant Gmail and Phone Notifications

Empowering swift action, our platform automatically dispatches notifications to designated administrators and responders, including emergency services like GTPD, the moment a fire incident is detected, enabling rapid response and mitigation efforts.
